Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. student of PRISDL in the School of Electronic, Electrical and Communication Engineering, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences , advised by Prof. Qixiang Ye.

My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, specifically for weakly supervised learning, feature learning, and visual object detection.
